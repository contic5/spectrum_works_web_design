Ride Rating System

The Thrill Rating System tells you how intense each ride is. The ratings are as follows

  1. Children's Ride
  2. Mild Thrill Ride
  3. Moderate Thrill Ride
  4. High Thrill Ride
  5. Aggressive Thrill Ride

Warning about the ride rating system

The ratings for aggressive thrill ride are a bit inaccurate. Sooper doper looper has the same rating as Candymonium, despite the fact that their intensity is far different.

Because of that, you want to watch the ride before you go on it. You can also look up the ride's max height, speed and inversions online.

Price

One day tickets are typically $92.80, but there are often discounts. Junior tickets (3-8) and senior tickets (62+) are $54.95. Tickets for two days are a bit cheaper for each day and visiting for two days will help you get everything done more easily.

Official Resorts

Hershey has three official resorts that you can check into in order to maximize your fun. Staying at a resort gets you better prices on park tickets and free shuttle services to the park. You can also get more time as well. Staying at a resort offers 1 hour early access and up to 3 hours evening access a. The resorts are also quite nice to stay at as well, and include pools.

Extra Features

Season Pass

The Season Pass includes unlimited visits. There are three versions, the Bite Size Season Pass, the Full Size Season Pass and the King Size Season Pass. The Season Pass is a great deal if you plan on visiting at least twice. Regular tickets cost around $90 while the Season Pass costs $160. There are other features included as well, such as 50% off parking.

The Full Size Pass is $200, but offers more discounts, free parking and 1 hour early access. The 1 hour early access makes it easier to get on rides.

The King Size Pass is $240. The main feature is that you can get free tickets. You can give these tickets to visiting friends or family. Tickets are about $76.95, which allows your friends or family to save a lot of money.

Fast Track

Fast Track is a way of spending extra money to skip lines on particiating attractions. Fast Track can be used once per ride. The cost ranges from $50-$145.

Fast Track Unlimited can be used an unlimited number of times per ride, but it is even more expensive. The cost ranges from $65-$199.
