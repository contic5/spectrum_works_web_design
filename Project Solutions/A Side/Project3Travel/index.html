American Dream is a mega-mall in East Rutherford, NJ. It has a wide variety of stores that you can visit. This mall is much larger than normal and additionally offers various unique attractions, including:

  • Nickelodeon Universe-Nickelodeon Universe Theme Park is the biggest indoor theme park in the Western Hemisphere. Experience the tallest, the steepest, and the longest rides in the world.
  • Dreamworks Water Park-DreamWorks Water Park is the largest indoor water park in North America. Experience year-round splashy fun with the largest selection of water slides worldwide.
  • Blacklight Minigolf-Blacklight Mini Golf is an indoor mini golf course that lets you putt through 18 holes of vibrant alien landscapes, uncharted planets, fun spaceships, and robots.
  • Mirror Maze-Mirror Maze is an amazing family adventure. Orbit you way into a new dimension filled with incite reflections.
  • Angry Birds Minigolf-Experience the world of Angry Birds via our one-of-a-kind Angry Birds Mini Golf course - all ages are guaranteed to have the time of their lives.
  • Big Snow-North America's first and only indoor, real-snow, year-round ski and snow resort. Every day is a snow day!
  • The Rink-Indoor ice skating at The Rink. A premier, NHL regulation sized ice skating rink offering public skating and special events.
  • Legoland-Discover millions of LEGO bricks and epic attractions for budding builders inside the ultimate indoor playground.
  • Sea Life-Explore the city under the sea! An underwater world featuring thousands of fish, sharks, rays and more amidst NYC-themed exhibits.
  • TILT-Tilt Museum is an original 3D street chalk museum from Tracy Lee Sum that lets you step into a life-size mural. Be part of the art.
