Nickelodeon Universe

Basic Info

Nickelodeon Universe offers a wide variety of attractions, from attractions that are extreme to ones that are fun for kids.

Nickeloden Universe also offers group events and birthday parties.

You can also go watch live character entertainment and purchase souveniers.

If you find excessive waits to be a hassle, you can purchase Nick QuickPass, which can be used an unlimited number of times for the park's top thrill rides.
