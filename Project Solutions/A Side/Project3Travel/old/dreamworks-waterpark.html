Dreamworks Waterpark

Basic Info

Dreamworks Waterpark offers the world's largest heated indoor wave pool at 1.6 million gallons.

Dreamworks Waterpark offers attractions great for little ones and for thrill seekers.

If you find excessive waits to be a hassle, you can purchase Nick QuickPass, which can be used an unlimited number of times for the waterpark's top thrill rides.

The temperature is maintained at a consistent 81 degrees the whole year.

You can rent an upscale private skybox suite or a poolside cabana for after you're done swimming.

Dreamworks Waterpark offers birthday parties as well.
