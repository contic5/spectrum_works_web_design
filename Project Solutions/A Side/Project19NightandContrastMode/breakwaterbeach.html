Breakwater Beach

Ride descriptions are from https://casinopiernj.com/breakwater-beach-attractions/

Mild Rides

The Perfect Storm

You will find something for everyone in this totally interactive, multi-level area. Under 6 years old must be accompanied by an adult. While in the Perfect Storm, watch out for the Nantucket Bucket which may dump 800 gallons of water on you!

Revolutionary Wild River

With depth charges and cannons being shot off, you will be surprised at every turn.

Moderate Rides

The Harbor

The Wave Pool at Breakwater Beach is approximately 5860 square feet and located at the far eastern end of the Waterpark, underneath the mountain golf course. There is a zero-depth entry sloping down to a maximum depth of 5’8” feet. There will be a rope of delineation (float line) at the 8’ away from the back of the wave chamber wall that will keep guests from going past. The wave pool uses pneumatic forced air pressure to create breaking waves repeatedly for 10 minutes, followed by a 10-minute rest period.

Two if by Sea

Two awesome rides off of one tower! Speed down the centrifugal force of the BulletBowl or embark on a surprise adventure that takes riders through a serpentine, enclosed tube with two funnel opening featuring gentle oscillation within the funnels and high banking turns along the ride.

Liberty Launch (Bullet BOWL)
The Bullet BOWL Tubing slide starts as an enclosed waterslide flume, which opens to a Bowl where guests experience a number of revolutions before exiting from the center of the Bowl, back into an enclosed flume and finally into a run out. Guests ride on either a double or single inflatable inner tube.

Freedom Flume (Topsy Turvy)
The Topsy-Turvy Tubing slide is an enclosed flume that transitions into different waterslide features throughout the course of the ride, adding a surprise element to the guest experience. Guests ride on either a double or single inflatable inner tube.

Patriot's Plunge

This 6 lane racing slide lets you compete against others as you plummet on a mat to the finish line. Grab a mat and race your friends.

Minuteman Express

The Minuteman Express is 2, 2-person tube slides. Guests ride a tube through twists and turns, ultimately splashing down at the bottom of the tower into a splash pool.

Intense Rides

Revere's Wild Ride

Take the twists and turns of a serpentine body slide as you wind your way above the lazy river!

Extreme Rides

Salem's Scream... Oh My Cod

Test your senses with the ALL NEW Aqua drop free fall! A 50 foot speed slide where the floor drops out from under you at the starting platform!!!
