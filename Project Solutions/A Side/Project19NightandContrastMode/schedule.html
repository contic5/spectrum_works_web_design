Casino Pier Schedule

Basic Summary of Schedule

Casino Pier is typically open from 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM from June 19 to August 31.

From June 1 to June 18, the park is typically open from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM.

Before then, Casino Pier is open on weekends from April through June.
