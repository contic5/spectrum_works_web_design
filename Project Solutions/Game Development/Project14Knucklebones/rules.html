About Cult of the Lamb

Knucklebones is a minigame from the game Cult of the Lamb

Cult of the Lamb is rated T for Teen.

Rules

Basics

Both players take turn rolling dice. Each player has a 3x3 grid. When a player rolls a dice, they place it in their own 3x3 grid. Dice can only be placed in empty squares.

Scoring

A player's score is the sum of all of their dice. When a value occurs twice in a column, it is multiplied by 2. When a value occurs three times in a columns, it is multiplied by 3.

Deleting Dice

When a player places a dice, the other player must remove all of their dice in the same column that match the current dice value.

Ending the Game

The game ends when one player completely fills up their board.

Strategy

From: https://www.thegamer.com/cult-of-the-lamb-knucklebones-guide-dice-minigame-strategy-ratau-shrumy-klunko-bop-flinky/

  • Get doubles or triples whenever you can.
  • Destroy opponent's dice whenever you can. Getting rid of doubles or triples is extremely important.
  • Place high dice in columns your opponent has already filled. They cannot get rid of your dice.
  • Place your ones and twos in their own column. This column should ideally match up with an opponent's empty column.
  • Try to place your ninth die first because you will most likely win in that case.
