What is Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art that focuses on improving both the body and mind. It is currently a popular martial art and has been featured at the Olympics.

Tae Kwon Do is made up of three parts

  1. Tae-Foot
  2. Kwon-Fist
  3. Do-Way

Taekwondo is more than just physical prowsess and skill. It is about improving mentally and making the world a better place. Strength gained through marital arts is meant to help others and to defend.

Taekwondo focuses heavily on feet rather than hands, unlike other martial arts. Taekwondo also connects its physical motions to mental aspects and life.

Taekwondo focuses on unity of body, mind and life. When doing Taekwondo, you need to focus on mental peace. Taekwondo is about following principles.
